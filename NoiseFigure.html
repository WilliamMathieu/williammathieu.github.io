Cascaded Noise Figure Calculator

In a receiver chain, noise from early stages dominates because later stages amplify it. The Friis noise formula shows the first stage contributes most to total NF — this is why an LNA at the front of a receiver is critical.

GₙGain of stage n (dB). Passive components have negative gain (insertion loss).
FₙNoise Figure of stage n (dB). A perfect noiseless component has NF = 0 dB.
Cascaded NFTotal system noise figure referred to the input.
Total gainSum of all stage gains (dB).
