Skin Depth Calculator

At RF frequencies, current concentrates near the conductor surface in a layer whose thickness (skin depth δ) decreases with frequency. This raises effective resistance at RF compared to DC. Conductor thickness should be ≥ 3–5 skin depths to minimise resistive loss.
