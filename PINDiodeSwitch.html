PIN Diode Switch

A PIN diode acts as a current-controlled RF resistor: forward-biased it is a small resistance Rf, reverse-biased it is a small junction capacitance Cj. Mounted in series with the line it passes the signal when forward-biased and blocks it when reverse-biased; mounted in shunt it does the opposite. This tool evaluates the two states of a single-diode SPST switch and reports the insertion loss and isolation for either topology.
