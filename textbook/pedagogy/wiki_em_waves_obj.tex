\begin{objectives}
\begin{itemize}[leftmargin=1.4em,itemsep=2pt]
\item Relate the electric and magnetic fields of a plane wave through the intrinsic impedance.
\item Compute wavelength, phase velocity, and time-average power density (the Poynting flux).
\item Distinguish linear, circular, and elliptical polarization.
\end{itemize}
