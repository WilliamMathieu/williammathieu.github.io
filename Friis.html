Friis Transmission Equation

The Friis equation gives the received power in a radio link as a function of transmitted power, antenna gains, distance, and wavelength. It assumes free-space propagation (line-of-sight, no reflections or atmospheric effects) and far-field conditions. It is the starting point for any link budget calculation.
