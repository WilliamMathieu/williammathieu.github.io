Total Capacitance

Compute the total capacitance of N capacitors in series or parallel. Used for designing resonant circuits and matching networks with available discrete components.

Series1/C_total = 1/C₁ + 1/C₂ + ... + 1/C_N. Total always smaller than any individual.
