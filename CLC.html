Capacitance in LC Circuit

Given an inductor and a target resonant frequency, calculates the required tuning capacitor. This is the core calculation for tuning RF coils, building RF filters, and designing matching networks.

Parameter reference ▸
LExisting inductor value.
fTarget resonant frequency (e.g. Larmor frequency for MRI).
CRequired capacitance = 1/(4π²f²L).
