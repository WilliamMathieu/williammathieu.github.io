Bandpass Filter Calculator

Computes L and C values for a series or parallel RLC bandpass resonator given centre frequency and bandwidth (or Q). Useful for IF filter design and narrowband matching.

Parameter reference ▸

BW−3 dB bandwidth. Q = f₀/BW.
RTermination resistance (Ω). Sets component values.
QQuality factor = f₀/BW. Higher Q → narrower, more selective filter.
SeriesResonant at f₀; presents low impedance in the passband.
ParallelResonant at f₀; presents high impedance in the passband.
