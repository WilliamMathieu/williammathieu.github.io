Antenna Array Factor

A phased array combines multiple antenna elements whose signals add constructively in the steered direction. λ/2 spacing is standard to avoid grating lobes.

NNumber of elements.
dElement spacing in wavelengths.
θ₀Steering angle (degrees from broadside).

Element spacing, d: λ
