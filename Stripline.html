Stripline Calculator

Stripline is a flat conductor sandwiched symmetrically between two ground planes. It is a fully shielded TEM medium — no dispersion, no radiation, lower loss than microstrip. Common in multilayer PCBs for RF routing and controlled-impedance backplanes.

Parameter reference ▸
wTrace width (mm).
bTotal substrate height (mm) — distance between the two ground planes.
tTrace thickness (mm).
εᵣDielectric constant of the filling material. FR4 ≈ 4.4.
Z₀Characteristic impedance. The trace is centred between the planes.
vₚPhase velocity = c/√εᵣ. Stripline is fully embedded so vₚ has no air correction.
