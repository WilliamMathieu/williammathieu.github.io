Multi-Turn RF Coil Designer

Extends the single-turn coil to a helical solenoid with N turns using the Wheeler short-solenoid formula (accurate to ~1%). Also computes wire resistance and quality factor Q — a critical figure of merit for MRI receive coils.

dWire diameter (mm).
DCoil winding diameter (mm).
lCoil axial length (mm).
NNumber of turns.
fLarmor frequency for tuning.
LInductance (Wheeler formula).
C_tuneTuning capacitor for resonance at f.
R_wireDC resistance (copper, ρ = 1.68×10⁻⁸ Ω·m). Actual RF resistance is higher due to skin effect.
QQuality factor = (1/R)·√(L/C). Higher Q = lower thermal noise = better SNR.
