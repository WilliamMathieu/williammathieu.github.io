Wavelength Calculator

Wavelength is the spatial period of a wave — the distance it travels per cycle. In a medium with dielectric constant εᵣ the wave travels at c/√εᵣ so the wavelength is shorter. Essential for antenna sizing and transmission line design.

fFrequency.
εᵣRelative dielectric constant. 1.0 for free space; higher for dielectrics.
λWavelength in the medium = c/(f·√εᵣ).
