Resonant Frequency of LC Circuit

At resonance, the inductive and capacitive reactances are equal and cancel. The circuit draws no net reactive power and presents a purely resistive impedance. Used in filters, oscillators, and RF matching networks.

Parameter reference ▸
LInductance. Stores energy in a magnetic field.
CCapacitance. Stores energy in an electric field.
f₀Resonant frequency = 1/(2π√LC). At this frequency ωL = 1/(ωC).
