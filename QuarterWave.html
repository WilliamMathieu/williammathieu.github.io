Quarter-Wave Transformer

A λ/4 transmission line section transforms impedance as Zin = Z₀²/ZL. Choosing Z₀ = √(Z₁·Z₂) gives a perfect match at the design frequency. Widely used in microwave circuits and antenna feed networks.
