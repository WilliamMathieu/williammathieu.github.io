S-Parameter Plotter

S-parameters (scattering parameters) describe how RF signals scatter at the ports of a network. S11 is the input reflection coefficient (return loss); S21 is the forward transmission (gain or insertion loss); S12 is reverse transmission; S22 is output reflection. They are measured at each frequency point with all other ports terminated in Z₀. Touchstone (.s1p, .s2p, etc.) is the standard file format exported by VNAs (vector network analysers).

S11Input reflection coefficient. |S11|² = fraction of input power reflected. -10 dB S11 means 10% of power is reflected.
S21Forward transmission. For a filter: passband loss and stopband rejection. For an amplifier: gain.
S12Reverse isolation. How much signal leaks backward through the device.
S22Output reflection coefficient — same as S11 but at the output port.
VSWRVoltage Standing Wave Ratio derived from |S11|. VSWR = (1+|S11|)/(1−|S11|).
Return Loss−20·log₁₀(|S11|) in dB. Larger (more negative) is better.

Supports Touchstone files: .s1p, .s2p, .s3p, .s4p
