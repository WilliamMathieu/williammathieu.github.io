Microstrip Calculator

Microstrip is the most common planar RF/microwave transmission line — a trace on top of a dielectric with a ground plane below. Uses Hammerstad-Jensen closed-form equations, accurate to <0.5%.

hSubstrate height (mm) — trace to ground plane.
wTrace width (mm).
tTrace thickness (mm). Typical PCB copper: 35 µm (1 oz), 70 µm (2 oz).
εᵣSubstrate dielectric constant. FR4 ≈ 4.4, Rogers 4003C ≈ 3.55, PTFE ≈ 2.1.
Z₀Characteristic impedance (Ω). Standard: 50 Ω RF, 75 Ω video.
εeffEffective dielectric constant — fringing fields extend partly into air.
vpPhase velocity = c/√εeff.
