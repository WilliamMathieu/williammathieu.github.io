Yagi-Uda Antenna

The Yagi-Uda antenna achieves directional gain through a driven dipole, a reflector element, and one or more directors. Gain scales approximately as 10 log₁₀(N) for large arrays. This calculator uses the NBS/Viezbicke empirical tables (Tech Note 688) optimised for d/λ = 0.0085 element diameter.
