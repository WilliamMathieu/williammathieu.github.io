Dielectric Loss Tangent

The loss tangent measures how lossy a dielectric is ‚ÄĒ the ratio of its imaginary to real permittivity. From it you get the dielectric Q, the attenuation a wave suffers travelling through the material, and the wavelength and velocity inside it. Enter the relative permittivity, loss tangent and frequency; add a length to get the total dielectric loss.
