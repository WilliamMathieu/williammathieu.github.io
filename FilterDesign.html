LC Filter Designer (Butterworth / Chebyshev)

LC ladder filters are lossless passive filters with alternating series inductors and shunt capacitors. Butterworth = flattest passband. Chebyshev = ripple in passband but steeper roll-off. Each additional order adds ~20 dB/decade of roll-off.

nFilter order. Higher order = steeper roll-off.
fcCutoff frequency (−3 dB for Butterworth; ripple edge for Chebyshev).
R₀Source/load impedance (Ω). Standard: 50 Ω.
RippleChebyshev only: peak-to-peak passband ripple (dB). Typical: 0.1–1 dB.
